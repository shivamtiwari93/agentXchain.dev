# Reminder Policy

## Reminder Cadence

Teams define a single reminder hour. Reminder previews describe who still owes standup for the requested date and which channel the reminder should reference.

## Escalation Rules

A missing standup is an operational issue, not an application crash. The product surfaces missing members in both the summary and reminder preview so operators can follow up.

## Quiet Hours

The example does not schedule real background jobs, but the team timezone and reminder hour are preserved so future automation can avoid sending reminders outside local working hours.
