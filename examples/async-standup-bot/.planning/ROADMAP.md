# Async Standup Bot Roadmap

## Goal

Ship a repo-local B2B standup collector that proves AgentXchain can govern team administration, integrations, operations, and QA.

## Delivery Slices

1. Team administration: create teams and manage members.
2. Standup submission: collect one update per member per day with blocker status.
3. Summary surface: produce a manager digest with missing-member visibility.
4. Reminder surface: preview reminder messages for missing submissions.
5. Operations: retention pruning and runbook coverage.
