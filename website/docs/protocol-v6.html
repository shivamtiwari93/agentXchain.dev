v6 Highlights

Scope Contracts Events Hooks

What v6 adds

  • Multi-repo coordinator config in agentxchain-multi.json
  • Coordinator command surface: multi init, multi step, multi approve-gate, multi resync
  • Coordinator history with acceptance_projection and context_generated events
  • Cross-repo context artifacts and informational context_invalidations
  • Coordinator hook phases: before_assignment, after_acceptance, before_gate, on_escalation

Coordinator hooks

The v6 hook payload contract includes super_run_id, workstream_id, repo_id, repo_run_id, pending_barriers, pending_gate, and phase-specific data like projection_ref and context_invalidations.

Blocking phases are before_assignment and before_gate. Advisory phases are after_acceptance and on_escalation.
