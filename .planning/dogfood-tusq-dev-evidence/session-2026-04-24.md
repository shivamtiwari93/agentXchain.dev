### v2.155.9 Dogfood Retry — GAP-007

Rejected turn_9b69c2bed31d203a, unblocked escalation, restarted continuous
loop with `agentxchain@2.155.9`. PM re-dispatched and produced output.

**Result**: schema validation FAILED again:

```text
acceptTurn(pm): Validation failed at stage schema:
  idle_expansion_result.vision_traceability must be an array.;
  idle_expansion_result.new_intake_intent must be an object.
```

**Root cause (GAP-007)**: the backward-compatible normalization from v2.155.8
only ran for sidecar-loaded results. When `idle_expansion_result` is embedded
directly in `turn-result.json`, `maybeAttachIdleExpansionSidecar()` returned
early at the `turnResult?.idle_expansion_result !== undefined` check without
calling `normalizeIdleExpansionSidecar()`. Schema validation then rejects the
un-normalized object-shaped `vision_traceability` and flat `new_intake_intent`
fields.

Additionally, the charter text baked into the dispatch is from v2.155.7
(object-shaped `vision_traceability`, flat `new_intake_intent` fields) because
the intake intent was created under that version. The PM faithfully follows
the charter and produces the old format, relying on normalization that never runs.

**Fix** (committed at `e3f16489`):
1. `maybeAttachIdleExpansionSidecar` now normalizes embedded
   `idle_expansion_result` in-place via `normalizeIdleExpansionSidecar()`
   before returning.
2. Added flat `new_intake_intent` extraction: when the PM puts `title`,
   `charter`, `acceptance_contract`, `priority`, `template` at the top level
   instead of nesting under `new_intake_intent`, the normalizer extracts them.

Shipping `agentxchain@2.155.10` with this fix.
